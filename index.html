THE IOT ONE ACADEMY

Digital Courses on the INDUSTRIAL INTERNET OF THINGS (IIoT) with real USE CASES from the SMARTFACTORY KUNSHAN (China).

About

The IOT ONE ACADEMY is a digital campus for corporate education of employees of companies in the field of Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT), also called Industry 4.0 in Germany.

The goal of the particularly practice-oriented, digital academy is to support large companies in modernizing parts of their production and administration with the latest IoT-Technologies.
The IOTONE ACADEMY uses the latest didactic, app-based methods to enable particularly sustainable learning and a high level of learning transfer.

  • We are the exclusive partner of the IoT One platform from China, which collects and presents use cases in the field of Internet 4.0.
  • We are a close partner of SmartFactory in Kunshan (near Shanghai), where more than 30 German SMEs have already very successfully set up their own production lines in order to be able to produce and sell locally.
  • We organize Learning Journeys for companies to Kunshan in order to show IIoT technologiesin action on site.
  • We then offer ideation workshops to develop IIoT applications for the participants'companies.

The technological basis of the IOT ONE Academy is the mobile learning platform of repeatmobile, a SaaS provider for app-based learning with customers such as Lufthansa, LSG SkyChefs, Volkswagen and Media Saturn.

HOW WE DO IT

With the IOT ONE ACADEMY we cover IIoT topics like Blockchain, Augmented Reality, AI, Machine Learning, Big Data, Smart Industry, etc. and make them easy to understand for employees of companies.

We also want to democratize the knowledge about Deep Tech to make it accessible to many target groups and therefore offer our courses in two party, a free and a non-free part:

  • General information via Multi Device (free content on normal PCs & Notebooks)
  • Special information and certificates via App (content subject to charge)

The courses fit into the demanding everyday lives of managers and employees and have a lasting influence on the knowledge and learning transfer of course participants.

What do you take home with you?

After completing the course, the participants have gained a high understanding of the advantages of IIoT technology in general and valuable eagle perspectives in particular and have also received good initial ideas about implementing IIoT technologies for their own company.

What is the duration?

The courses have a duration of 6-8 weeks.

What price?

The price per person is 2,750 EUR with a minimum of 5 participants. We offer graduated prices for higher numbers of participants.

WHY

Why do we do this?

In terms of digitization, Germany is far behind in the B2C sector - as are many other western countries. The market, which is aimed at consumers (such as Facebook, Twitter, Whatsapp, etc.) is divided between Americans and Chinese. In the B2B sector and especially in the digitalisation of industry (also known as Industry 4.0), however, Germany still has a significant chance of maintaining its top position in the world market. But hurry is called for, there is not much time left. We want to support and help to ensure that our German economy will continue to be efficient and competitive in the future: by providing well-founded information on IIoT topics, comprehensively but easily digestible and presented in an exciting way, with a high practical relevance and a high monetary take-away value.

METHOD

The IOT ONE ACADEMY uses an app-centric learning method that is used and proven in many companies today for the extra-occupational training of employees. For example, managers from management and IT departments can be trained sustainably in certain topics by using the learning app for only about 10-15 minutes per day. Learning in the app is fun and also meets the human needs of highly stressed employees.

Digital courses include:

Specific IIoT topics (e.g. Augmented Reality, Blockchain, AI, ML etc.)
Training along real existing IIoT-use cases
Overviews / eagle's perspective and easy to understand summaries
High practical relevance in all contents
Workshop situations with tutors (real or virtual)

Didactic implementation of a course (with 4-8 individual trainings):

Deep insights made easy to understand
Digestible content, didactically structured
Simple adaptation to the often "brutal" everyday life of a manager
Supported by an editorial team and a Messenger system in Whats app style
Entertainment in content and formats (infotainment)
Equipped with the latest App Learning Technology (powered by repeatmobile, a pioneer in mobile learning)

What is the procedure?

Courses take place with a minimum of 5 participants
Participants register independently on the academy page and receive a login by e-mail.
In case of payment, the entire course will be activated + a seminar date on site (or virtually) will bearranged.
Participants download the app from the Internet and start digital training.
The course is held, paying participants receive editorial support & a certificate.
An Ideation Workshop takes place in the seminar
Ideas for the integration in the company are generated and follow-ups are planned.

PARTNER

SMARTFACTORY KUNSHAN

Smart Factory Kunshan is a Startup Factory joint venture with the Kunshan City Government, which was established in 2017 specifically for Industry 4.0. The official opening of the 15,000 square meter facility will take place in October 2018, but our Smart Factory Event Arena will open its doors in spring 2018.

The aim of the demonstration and integration factory is to create an innovative environment for Industrial Internet of Things / Industry 4.0 from research and demonstration to employee qualification. We enable international and Chinese companies to integrate and demonstrate their Industry 4.0 technologies into the real production environment of one of our companies.
